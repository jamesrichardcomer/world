5. **Quantum Gravity & Unification**
   - Reconciling quantum mechanics with general relativity remains unsolved; gravity absent from Standard Model.
   - String theory, loop quantum gravity, and other approaches seeking quantum description of spacetime.
   - Experiments manipulating gravitational waves with lasers may reveal gravity's quantum nature.
