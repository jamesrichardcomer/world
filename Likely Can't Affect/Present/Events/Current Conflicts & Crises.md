## Major Wars (10,000+ deaths/year)
**1. Russia-Ukraine War (2022–)**
- Largest European conflict since WWII
- ~350,000+ killed since 2022 (military and civilian combined)
- Russia controls ~20% of internationally recognized Ukraine
- 5+ million Ukrainian refugees abroad, ~4 million internally displaced
- Ukraine under military and diplomatic pressure to cede Donbas

**2. Sudan Civil War (2023–)**
- Sudanese Armed Forces (SAF) vs. Rapid Support Forces (RSF)
- World's largest humanitarian crisis by absolute numbers
- 150,000+ estimated civilian deaths as of late 2024; total likely much higher
- 12+ million displaced
- Country effectively partitioned: RSF holds Darfur/western Sudan, SAF holds center/east
- UAE supplying arms to RSF; famine conditions widespread

**3. Israel-Gaza / Middle East (2023–)**
- Escalated after October 7, 2023 Hamas attack
- 21,000+ killed between Aug 2024–Aug 2025 in Gaza alone
- Israel banned UNRWA from Palestine in Jan 2025
- Expanded into broader regional conflict: Israel struck Iran, Lebanon, Syria, Yemen
- Iran-Israel ceasefire took effect June 2025
- Gaza infrastructure largely destroyed

**4. Myanmar Civil War (1948–, escalated 2021–)**
- Longest civil war in the world
- Military coup in 2021 reignited full-scale violence
- 15,000+ killed between mid-2024 and mid-2025
- Most fragmented conflict globally: 1,200+ distinct armed groups
- Military capitalizing on foreign support to divide resistance

**5. Mexican Drug War (2006–)**
- Government vs. multiple drug trafficking cartels
- 8,600+ killed between mid-2024 and mid-2025
- Among top 10 most severe conflicts globally

## Other Active Conflicts (1,000–10,000 deaths/year)
- **Afghanistan** — Taliban vs. ISIL-K and other factions (post-US withdrawal)
- **DRC (Eastern Congo)** — Multiple armed groups, escalated 2024–2025
- **Ethiopia** — Tigray tensions re-escalating in early 2026; clashes between TDF and federal troops
- **Syria** — Post-Assad political competition, sectarian violence, foreign meddling; 9,000+ killed in past 12 months
- **Haiti** — Gang violence; 4,500+ killed from political violence
- **Nigeria** — Complex patchwork of regional conflicts
- **Pakistan** — Rising Islamist and separatist insurgencies; India-Pakistan exchange of fire in 2025 was worst in decades
- **Ecuador** — Gang violence escalating; 50+ armed groups active
- **Sahel region** (Mali, Burkina Faso, Niger) — Jihadist insurgencies expanding

## Major Tension Points / Watchlist for Escalation
- **Taiwan Strait** — Potential China-US flashpoint; rated even chance of crisis in 2026
- **Russia-NATO** — Risk of direct clashes rated as high impact
- **North Korea** — Rose to top tier concern for 2026
- **India-Pakistan** — Nuclear powers; 2025 clashes were worst in decades
- **Horn of Africa** — Ethiopia-Eritrea tensions could tip region into wider war
- **Red Sea** — Houthi disruption of shipping; regional power struggles
- **US domestic** — Political violence/unrest rated high-likelihood, high-impact for 2026

- Highest global conflict risk in decades
