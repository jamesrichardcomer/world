**Classification:** Humid Subtropical (Köppen: Cfa)

### Temperature Ranges
**Annual:** 52°F to 91°F (rarely below 39°F or above 94°F)

**Winter (Dec-Feb):**
- Highs: 68-73°F | Lows: 50-55°F
- Coldest month: January (avg 60.6°F)
- Freezing weather typically 1-2 times per winter
- Record low: -6.5°F (1977, 1981, 1983)
- Snow extremely rare (1977 slight accumulation, 2010 light flurries)

**Spring (Mar-May):**
- Highs: 77-86°F | Lows: 57-69°F
- Pleasant, warming, less humid than summer

**Summer (Jun-Sep):**
- Highs: 89-92°F | Lows: 74-77°F
- Hottest: Early August (92°F highs, 76°F lows)
- Hot, oppressive, wet, mostly cloudy
- 111 days/year above 90°F
- Rarely exceeds 100°F

**Fall (Oct-Nov):**
- Highs: 78-84°F | Lows: 60-68°F
- Similar to spring, pleasant

### Precipitation
**Annual:** 51.5 inches (1,305mm) | 188 rainfall days

**Wettest Months:** June-July (5-6 inches)
**Driest Months:** March, November, December (1.3-1.5 inches)

**Rainy Season:** June-September with frequent afternoon thunderstorms

### Humidity
**Average:** 74% year-round (rarely below 50%)
- Daily range: 89% (4am) to 55% (1pm)
- Highest: August (79%)
- Lowest: March-April (67-70%)
- Makes temperatures feel warmer than actual

### Sunshine
- **Annual:** 2,930 hours (66% of daylight hours)
- **Summer:** Long days (13h 58min in June)
- **Winter:** Short days (10h 20min in December)
- 89 days/year with mostly clear skies

### Hurricanes & Severe Weather
- **Hurricane Season:** June-November (peak: August-October)
- **Location Protection:** 42 miles inland from Atlantic, 77 miles from Gulf
- **Hurricane Probability:** 0.4% annual (Cat 3-5: 0.1%)
- **Tornadoes:** Possible May-October during thunderstorms (less dangerous than central US)
- **Notable:** Hit by 3 hurricanes in 2004 (Charley worst)

### Best Weather
**Fall/Winter/Spring:** September-May (except summer months)
- Less rain, moderate temperatures, lower humidity
