### Affluent/Upscale
1. **Winter Park**
   - Upscale suburb with cobblestone streets, Park Avenue shopping, fine dining, Rollins College
   - Charles Hosmer Morse Museum, chain of lakes, multi-million dollar homes
   - Most expensive homes, considered "nicest" area

2. **Windermere**
   - Richest suburb, lakefront mega-mansions on Butler Chain of Lakes
   - Median income: $156,042 | Home avg: $906,600+

3. **Dr. Phillips**
   - Restaurant Row on Sand Lake Road, close to I-4, Universal, convention hubs
   - Strong entertainment and hospitality connections

### Family-Friendly/Safe
4. **Lake Mary**
   - One of Florida's safest cities, low crime, highly-rated Seminole County schools
   - Tech/finance hub with corporate presence, International business park

5. **Oviedo**
   - Near UCF, excellent schools, family-oriented with strong youth sports culture
   - Historic downtown, known for free-roaming chickens (past), oak-shaded roads
   - Median home: $525K

6. **Belle Isle**
   - 4th best suburb, excellent schools, safe community

### Growing/Modern
7. **Lake Nona**
   - "Medical City" - tech and medical hub with smart infrastructure
   - Medical City, UCF College of Medicine, VA Hospital, autonomous shuttles
   - 8-9% annual price growth | Median: $790-800K
   - Proximity to airport and Disney developments

8. **Winter Garden**
   - Small-town feel, lively walkable downtown with shops, breweries, local charm
   - West Orange Trail for biking/golf carts

9. **Celebration**
   - Disney-designed master-planned community, movie-like aesthetic
   - Very walkable, safe, maintained, strong events/community vibe
   - Strict HOA, tourist presence in downtown

### Regional Suburbs
10. **Baldwin Park**
    - Mixed-use community inside Orlando, tree-lined streets, lakeside parks
    - Village-like feel, highly walkable | Median: $575K
    - Baldwin Park Elementary, Glenridge Middle

11. **Clermont**
    - Rolling hills (rare in Florida), National Training Center (Olympic training)
    - Lake access, growing fast, more affordable | Population tripled in decade

12. **Sanford**
    - Orlando Sanford International Airport location
    - Historic downtown, north of Orlando

13. **Kissimmee**
    - South of Orlando, tourism-adjacent, more affordable

### Other Notable Areas

- **Davenport** - Southwest, near Disney/Universal
