# Major Austin Roads & Georgetown Connections
## I. Primary Interstates & Freeways
### A. Interstate 35 (I-35)
- **Description:** The backbone of the region, running directly from Georgetown south through Austin.
    
- **Georgetown Context:** This is your primary direct route, but also the most congested.
    
- **Current Status (North):** The **Capital Express North** project is currently underway (until ~2028) between SH 45 North and US 290 East. Expect narrowed lanes and frontage road construction as they add high-occupancy vehicle (HOV) lanes.
    
- **Key Features:**
    
    - **The "Split" (Central):** Where I-35 meets SH 71/Ben White Blvd is a massive interchange.
        
    - **The Upper/Lower Decks (Central):** Between Airport Blvd and MLK Blvd. _Tip: Take the Upper Decks if you are just passing through downtown._
        

### B. Loop 1 (Mopac Expressway)
- **Description:** The primary alternative to I-35, serving West Austin and the Tech Corridor.
    
- **Georgetown Connection:** Accessible via **SH 45 North**. This is often the preferred route if you are heading to The Domain or Downtown Austin to avoid I-35 gridlock.
    
- **Key Features:**
    
    - **Mopac Express Lanes:** Variable-toll lanes in the center that guarantee a minimum speed.
        
    - **Northern Terminus:** Ends at SH 45; merging here can be tricky during rush hour as four lanes compress.
        

### C. US Highway 183 & 183A Toll
- **Description:** A major northwest-to-southeast diagonal corridor.
    
- **Georgetown Connection (183A):** The **183A Toll** runs through Leander and Cedar Park and connects to **Hero Way** and **SH 29** in West Georgetown.
    
- **Function:** The "Western Escape." If I-35 is parked, taking SH 29 West to 183A South is a reliable (though tolled) back door into North Austin.
    

### D. State Highway 45 North (SH 45)
- **Description:** An East-West toll loop located just south of Round Rock.
    
- **Function:** The critical "Crossover." It connects I-35, Mopac, and US 183.
    
- **Use Case:** Georgetown residents often take I-35 South, then hop on SH 45 West to reach Mopac or the Apple Campus, bypassing the worst traffic in North Austin (Wells Branch/Parmer area).
    

## II. The "Georgetown-to-Austin" Arterials
### A. Ronald Reagan Blvd / Parmer Lane
- **Description:** A massive north-south arterial. It is called **Ronald Reagan Blvd** in Georgetown/Leander and becomes **Parmer Lane** as it enters Austin.
    
- **Function:** The "Tech Corridor" Route.
    
- **Destinations:** Direct access to the **Apple Campus**, **The Domain** (via Mopac connection), and the **Austin FC Stadium**.
    
- **Traffic:** High speed (60+ mph) but has traffic lights. It is significantly less stressful than I-35 for reaching North West Austin.
    

### B. State Highway 130 (SH 130)
- **Description:** The high-speed eastern toll bypass (85 mph speed limit).
    
- **Georgetown Connection:** Runs along the east side of Georgetown (past the toll booths).
    
- **Use Case:** The absolute fastest route to **Austin-Bergstrom International Airport (AUS)** and the Tesla Gigafactory. It avoids 99% of Austin traffic but costs ~$8-12 one way.
    

### C. FM 1431 (University Blvd in Round Rock)
- **Description:** A major East-West connector linking I-35 (near IKEA) to 183A (Cedar Park).
    
- **Caution:** Highly congested near the I-35 intersection and the hospitals.
    

## III. Major Austin Surface Arterials
### A. North-South
1. **Lamar Boulevard:** The "Main Street" of Austin.
    
    - _North Lamar:_ Gritty, older retail, Asian markets (Chinatown Center).
        
    - _South Lamar:_ Trendy apartments, Alamo Drafthouse, heavy dining scene.
        
2. **Burnet Road:** Runs parallel to Mopac (East of it).
    
    - _Character:_ The "unofficial restaurant row" of North Austin. Great for finding local food without downtown prices.
        
    - _Q2 Stadium:_ Home of Austin FC, located near Burnet & Braker.
        
3. **Congress Avenue:** Leads directly to the Capitol; South Congress (SoCo) is the major tourist strip.
    

### B. East-West
1. **Anderson Lane:** A key connector in North Austin (between Mopac and Lamar) with high-quality local dining.
    
2. **Cesar Chavez & Riverside:** The main routes flanking Lady Bird Lake downtown.
